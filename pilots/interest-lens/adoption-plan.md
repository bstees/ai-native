# Interest Lens Adoption Plan

## Purpose

Describe how `Interest Lens` will act as the proving ground for the `AI Native`
service model.

## Near-Term Sequence

1. Curate signals into a small set of decision-worthy concepts.
2. Approve one or two concepts for asset creation.
3. Publish the first shared assets in `AI Native`.
4. Introduce those assets into `Interest Lens`.
5. Record what was easy, awkward, or unexpectedly valuable.

## First Asset Candidates

- reusable prompts
- repo rules
- validation checklists

## Success Signals

- `Interest Lens` adopts at least one shared asset with minimal translation
- the asset feels maintained rather than one-off
- adoption feedback helps improve both the asset and the sharing model
