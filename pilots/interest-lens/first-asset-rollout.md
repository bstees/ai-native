# Interest Lens First Asset Rollout

## Goal

Pilot the first shared asset from `AI Native` inside `Interest Lens`.

## Asset

- `assets/repo-rules/ai-native-core-operating-rules.md`

## Why This Asset First

- It is broadly reusable.
- It is easy to review with humans before adoption.
- It creates a stable baseline while the vendor ecosystem keeps shifting.

## Proposed Rollout

1. Copy or adapt the shared rules into the `Interest Lens` AI guidance area.
2. Add only the minimum repo-local specifics needed for stack, commands, and
   deployment boundaries.
3. Use the asset during normal development tasks for a short pilot period.
4. Record friction points, missing guidance, and over-constrained guidance.

## Human Gates

- Review the shared baseline before introducing it into `Interest Lens`.
- Review any repo-local edits that materially narrow or broaden permissions.
- Review pilot feedback before promoting the asset as a broader default.

## Feedback To Capture

- What translated cleanly
- What needed repo-specific adjustment
- What was too vague
- What was too restrictive
- Whether the asset actually reduced duplicated prompt/rule work
