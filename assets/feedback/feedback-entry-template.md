# Feedback Entry Template

## Metadata

- `feedback_id`:
- `captured_on`:
- `captured_by`:
- `source_type`: `toil` | `developer-note` | `audit`
- `scope`: `repo-local` | `candidate-shared`
- `status`: `new` | `triaged` | `promoted` | `closed`

## Context

What work was happening when this feedback appeared.

## Friction

What toil, confusion, inconsistency, or usability problem occurred.

## Likely Guidance Gap

What missing instruction, workflow, checklist, or standard would have reduced
the problem.

## Proposed Improvement

What should be added, changed, or clarified.

## Evidence

Optional links to commits, screenshots, tasks, or related markdown.

## Next Action

What should happen during the next ingestion review.
